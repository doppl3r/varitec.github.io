Sales Engineer

Post Date: 12/14/2016

Send resume to

cheryll@varitecsolutions.com

Description Varitec Solutions is seeking an outside sales engineer in Phoenix, Albuquerque and El Paso to conduct the multi-level selling efforts required to successfully produce orders for large capital HVAC equipment. These efforts include calling on mechanical and general contractors, mechanical engineering consultants, and building owners, developing professional and personal relationships with key personnel at these firms, and utilizing these relationships to produce highly profitable orders for Varitec.

Responsibilities

  • Call on assigned accounts and develop relationships with key personnel.
  • Work closely with assigned accounts to develop mechanical system design concepts.
  • Develop and conduct presentations to groups of clients.
  • Develop relationships with factory personnel, host factory visitors and organize seminars.
  • Maintain a working knowledge of assigned product and manufacturers.
  • Stay up to date and familiar with factory contacts, products, model numbers, descriptions, etc.
  • Prepare and price proposals from a set of construction documents, plans & specs, including a review of the project bid form.
  • Make a timely response to all customer inquiries.

Qualifications include:

  • Proficiency with Windows applications including Word, Excel and Power Point. Auto CAD experience is a plus.
