Fan and Air Distribution Project Manager

Post Date: 06/05/2017

Send resume to

cheryll@varitecsolutions.com

Description Varitec’s Phoenix office is looking for a project manager to service customers and contribute to the profitable results of the Sales Team by providing accurate and timely submittals, order releases, and project coordination.

Responsibilities

  • While working closely with the outside sales staff, prepare timely and accurate submittals for all Fan and Air Distribution Products from onscreen takeoff programs or manually with hard documents.
  • Release orders to various manufacturers in timely manner to meet the customer’s needs and project requirements.
  • Maintain and improve project profitability through the application of good project management techniques and skill development.
  • Participate in project coordination meetings where critical milestones are established and commitments made to customers are communicated to the project team.
  • Be able to determine equal products from Varitec’s manufacturers and the competition.
  • Communicate to management when pricing is not in line with market price and local product knowledge.
  • Communicate well with the manufacturers Varitec represents and the customers we have earned.
  • Prioritize your workload to best serve our clients
  • Have a thorough working knowledge of required products.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Qualifications include:

  • A minimum of a high school diploma is required, some college preferred.
  • Mechanical Aptitude.
  • Previous Sheet Metal HVAC takeoff or installation experience is a positive.
  • Computer skills and proficiency with manufactures’ software programs, takeoff software, Outlook, Word and Excel is required.
  • Ability to read, analyze, and interpret construction documents including drawings and specifications.
  • Excellent organization skills, accuracy, dependable work habits and good communication skills.
  • Ability to compose articulate, grammatically correct, and effective written communication.
  • Speak articulately one-on-one or to small and medium sized groups.
  • HVAC experience preferred

We Offer:

  • A Competitive Compensation Plan
  • Medical/ Dental/ Vision/Life, 401K with generous Employer Match, Vacation and paid Holidays
  • Career growth opportunities
