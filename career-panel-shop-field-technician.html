Panel Shop Field Technician

El Paso, Phoenix & Tucson

Post Date: 03/30/2018

Send resume to

cheryll@varitecsolutions.com

Description The Panel Shop Field Technician will serve as a representative of Varitec. The goal of the Technician is to provide an exceptional “owning experience” for the end user and contractor. Install, Variable Frequency Drive (VFD)/Control Panel Start-up and commission, troubleshooting of mainly commercial HVAC electrical equipment including troubleshooting and repair issues with equipment upon arrival from the factory, during and after the warranty period.

Responsibilities

  • Work as a team with the UL Panel Shop Supervisor and Manager to fulfill customer needs with courtesy in a timely manner.
  • Perform control panel, VFD, install and startup of Varitec UL Panel Shop control panels and control panels by others, and equipment troubleshooting and repair as required by the project.
  • Perform mechanical HVAC equipment install and repair for Varitec Equipment Sales as required
  • Coordination of Varitec activities or its suppliers as well as with the mechanical, the general & often the owner.
  • Interface with Varitec Aftermarket Department to support their service needs as part of their selling activities.
  • Become familiar with factory personnel, product, model numbers, descriptions, etc.
  • Work in Varitec UL508A Panel shop, manufacturing, testing, packaging control panels when not needed in the field

Qualifications include:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent required.
  • Minimum of two (2) years’ experience in the electrical field. UL508A control panel install/service experience is a plus.
  • Ability to read wiring schematics of all kinds, i.e. ladder grams, wiring diagrams, etc. a must.
  • VFD programming experience, a plus
  • Troubleshooting experience with digital multi-meter, voltage testers, amp meters/probes, etc., a must.
  • Ability to troubleshoot live, three phase powered equipment a must, following NEC 70E arc flash guidelines. Knowledge of proper PPE required
  • Ability to work with all types of conduit and wire; EMT bending experience a must, work with rigid, flexible conduit types. THHN, MTW, multi-conductor cables, etc.
  • Working knowledge of commercial HVAC machinery and their application a plus.
  • Ability to recon, plan, quote assist, purchase all needed parts, etc. for field installs.
  • Excellent customer service/communication skills required.
  • Ability to use Windows applications primarily Word, Excel, and Outlook.
  • Valid driver’s license
