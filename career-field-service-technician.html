Field Service Technician

El Paso, Phoenix & Tucson

Post Date: 05/04/2016

Send resume to

cheryll@varitecsolutions.com

Description Serve as a representative of Varitec Solutions; the rising leader of HVAC Solutions. The goal of the Technician is to provide an exceptional “owning experience” for the end user and contractor. Start-up and commission of HVAC equipment and controls including troubleshooting and repair issues with equipment upon arrival from the factory or during the warranty period.

Responsibilities

  • Work as a team with the Sales Staff to fulfill customer needs with the utmost professionalism and courtesy in a timely manner.
  • Perform start up, owner training, or equipment troubleshooting and repair as required by the project.
  • Coordination of Varitec activities or its suppliers as well as with the mechanical, the general & often the owner.
  • Interface with Varitec Aftermarket Department to support their service needs as part of their selling activities.
  • Become familiar with factory personnel, product, model numbers, descriptions, etc.
  • Many others, not listed

Qualifications include:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent required.
  • Minimum of ten (10) years’ experience in the HVAC industry. HVAC Service experience is a plus.
  • R-410A Refrigerant Handling License
  • Government Clearances are a plus (At a minimum; must be able to qualify for clearances)
  • OSHA Safety Certifications are a plus – We are a Safety First Environment.
  • In depth working knowledge of general HVAC equipment, VRV’s, VFD’s Controls, tools and their application.
  • Excellent customer service/communication skills are required.
  • Ability to use Windows applications primarily Word, Excel, and Outlook.
  • Valid driver’s license

  • Career growth opportunities
