Building Quality UL508A Panels from the Ground Up

In operation since the 1990’s, Varitec’s Electrical Control and Automation division manufactures UL508A electrical panels for a variety of industries and applications. In 2009 our business expanded into a new assembly line style production facility, providing the ability to assemble high quality panels with increased efficiencies and reduced production times. Today, Varitec is equipped to manufacture any size, type or style of UL508A enclosed electrical control panel to meet your needs.

When you need individual custom panels or large quantity production runs, Varitec is ready to deliver. We use PROMIS-E and AutoCAD LT software to design and lay out panels to your specifications. Our innovative designs utilize the latest technologies, such as Hoffman WeatherFlow enclosures for outdoor conditions, or PLC controls for precision management of your systems.
